Our Mission

At UtahRunning.com we fuel runners' passion. We aim to be the premier online destination for Utah runners by:

  • Building a comprehensive resource and community for runners, coaches, race directors and event coordinators
  • Inspiring more people in Utah to run and live a healthier lifestyle

Utah Running Races

It is certainly no wonder that Utah is one of the premiere destinations in the world for people who love the outdoors.

Many of Utah's races take place in some of the most beautiful natural surroundings in the country. For this reason, it is no surprise that Utah running races have been growing fast in popularity.

Races include everything from a Utah Marathon to 5k races, 10k races, Half Marathons and much more.

Club Mission Statement

The Utah Running Club will inspire and motivate runners through camaraderie and maintained focus on personal improvement. It will provide runners in Utah with a positive, team atmosphere and support in their pursuit of a healthy lifestyle.
