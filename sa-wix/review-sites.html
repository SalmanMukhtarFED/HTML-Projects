How to Manage Review Sites

Manage your online reputation by using this tool to collect reviews on Shopper Approved, Facebook, and Google My Business.

Direct a percentage of your review traffic to the sites that matter most. Authorize your Facebook and Google pages.

  • - Add the percentage of reviews you'd like to direct to each review site.
  • - Change it any time to focus traffic on increasing your star rating or number of reviews
  • - Set to 0% to pause traffic from being sent to Facebook or Google.
