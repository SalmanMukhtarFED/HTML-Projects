View Sites - GET

https://www.shopperapproved.com/api/sites.php?key=[api_key]

Edit Variables:

active-
1 active, 0 inactive
label -
The domain or company name of a merchant.
domain -
The domain of the merchant. Required if the company name is used for label.
require_orderid -
Only accept full reviews if the order id was assigned in the link.
foreign_id -
The ID your site uses for the account.
seal_header -
The header text on the seal.
date_format -
The date format on the seal. This is in php date format.
seal_color -
The seal color (blue, black, gold, green, red)
is_service -
(0 or 1) If set then the review will have service oriented questions.
widget_type -
The reviews on the widgets (5, 4-5, picked)
featured_days -
The life span of hand picked reviews.
hide_links -
The number of days that links on the certificates will be hidden for new reviews.
hide_reviews -
The number of days a review is private.
certificate_link -
Include a link to write a review on the certificate.
reviews_on_certificate -
The number of reviews on each certificate page.
default_delivery_days -
The default days between initial survey and follow up email.

Email Alerts:

email_all -
Email all full survey reviews.
email_low -
Email 1-2 star full survey reviews.
email_medium -
Email 3 star full survey reviews.
email_high -
Email 4-5 star full survey reviews
email_initial_all -
Email all initial survey reviews.
email_initial_low -
Email 1-2 star initial survey reviews.
email_initial_medium -
Email 3 star initial survey reviews.
email_initial_high -
Email 4-5 star initial survey reviews.
email_video -
Email after a video has been submitted.
